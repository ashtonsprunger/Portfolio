ABOUT

Hi, my name is Ashton Sprunger. In 2020, I graduated from highschool and Eleven Fifty Academy. Eleven Fifty is a three month long, immersive, full time coding bootcamp. I took their web development course which taught me HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and more advanced concepts.

I grew up second in line out of 8 kids, currently ranging from nineteen years old, all the way down to two years old. My dad teaches technology at Carroll and my mom homeschools us. Us kids have always been trained to work hard and efficiently.

My list of hobbies is huge and always changing. One of them being musical instruments and writing songs on the piano. I am always learning more about music and picking up new skills as I go, such as the ukulele. I also love anything mechanical including, building things, fixing clocks, and more! Other interests of mine include, photography, video editing, volleyball and most recently, coding!
